About BorneoNXT

BorneoNXT is a festival celebrating the fusion of technology, startups, culture, music, and creativity. Inspired by the global SXSW, our event brings together the brightest minds and most innovative ideas, rooted in the unique identity of Borneo. We aim to position Sarawak and TEGAS Digital Village as a vibrant hub for both innovation and creative expression. Join us to explore, connect, and co-create the future.
